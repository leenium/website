About Leenium

Leenium started as an Omarchy-derived setup and evolved into a complete Arch + Hyprland environment with a reproducible install flow.

Approach

Ship a coherent baseline instead of expecting users to assemble one from scratch.

Behavior

Keyboard-first defaults, discoverable keybindings, and practical helper commands.

Maintenance

leenium-update for regular updates, with recovery commands when state drifts.
